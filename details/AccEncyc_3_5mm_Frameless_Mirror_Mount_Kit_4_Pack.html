Premium Frameless Mirror Mount Kit

This upgraded frameless mirror mounting kit is designed to provide maximum security and durability for your mirror installation. Featuring a dual-screw fixing system, it offers twice the support of traditional single-screw mounts, ensuring your mirror stays stable and firm without wobbling or tilting. The kit comes complete with 8 heavy-duty 35mm screws and 22mm anchors, built to hold frameless mirrors up to 3-5mm in thickness safely and securely. The chrome-treated clamps add a sleek, polished aesthetic that blends effortlessly with both modern and traditional home decor. With its user-friendly design, installation is quick and easy, allowing you to set up your mirror mount in just a few minutes. Non-slip plastic pads are included to prevent your mirror from shifting on the wall and provide an additional layer of safety.
