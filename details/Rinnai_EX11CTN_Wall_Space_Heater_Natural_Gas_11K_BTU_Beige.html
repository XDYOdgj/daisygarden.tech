Rinnai EX11CTN Wall Space Heater

This powerful and efficient wall-mounted heater is designed to provide consistent warmth for small to medium spaces in your home, apartment, or commercial setting. With its direct vent system and natural gas operation, it eliminates the need for traditional ductwork, making installation simpler and more flexible.

Equipped with a seven-stage modulating gas valve, the Rinnai EX11CTN delivers precise temperature control, ensuring your space stays at the perfect level. The electronic spark ignition system makes it easy to start and operate, while the forced convection technology improves heat distribution and enhances comfort throughout the room.

For added convenience, this heater includes an enameled tray humidifier that helps maintain optimal indoor humidity, especially during colder months. The compact design allows it to fit seamlessly into walls, making it an ideal solution where floor or ceiling heating is not practical.

With features like child lock and a self-diagnostic system that automatically shuts down and restarts in case of malfunction, safety is a priority. The energy-efficient design also means you can enjoy lower utility bills, making this heater a smart and reliable investment for year-round comfort.
