LuxHeat 80 Sqft Electric Floor Heating System Kit

Bring warmth to your floors with the LuxHeat electric floor heating system. Designed for easy installation, this innovative kit provides a seamless and efficient way to heat any room, whether it's your bathroom, kitchen, or bedroom. The self-adhesive mat is built with dual-sided tape, ensuring a secure and flat installation that works under various flooring materials like tile, laminate, and stone.

Key Features

  • Easy-to-install 80 sqft self-adhesive heating mat
  • Included programmable thermostat with GFCI and floor sensor
  • Customizable coverage with 3" pre-spaced mat that can be cut, rotated, or flipped
  • Dual wire technology for ultra-low EMF emissions and long-term durability
  • Built-in cable monitor and alarm for enhanced safety and reliability
