Experience the Ultimate Comfort with the WarmlyYours Heated Floor Mat Kit

Transform your bathroom or any cold room into a warm, inviting space with this 85 sqft heated floor system. Ideal for those seeking added comfort and energy efficiency, the WarmlyYours kit provides consistent heat throughout your home. Featuring an advanced UTN5 thermostat with an LED display and capacitive touch, it allows for effortless temperature adjustments without the need for complicated programming.

Every component of this system is thoughtfully designed to ensure optimal performance. The included circuit tester simplifies the installation process, while the two high-precision floor sensors guarantee even heat distribution across your space. Whether you're installing under tile, stone, laminate, or luxury vinyl plank, the ultra-thin 1/8-inch mat ensures minimal disruption to your floor level, making it an ideal choice for modern homes.

With a 25-year warranty and UL certification for wet areas, this heated floor mat kit is built to last. It is also equipped with built-in GFCI protection and a child-lock feature for added safety, especially in homes with young children. The 15W/sqft radiant heating system quickly delivers comfortable warmth, making it suitable for use in even the most humid environments.
