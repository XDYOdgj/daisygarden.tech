Introducing the Cadet Com-Pak Twin Wall Heater

Designed for quick and efficient heating in large spaces, this robust wall heater offers powerful performance with a 3000W capacity. Whether you're heating a home or workplace, it delivers consistent warmth and comfort.

With a built-in thermostat, you can easily control the temperature to suit your preferences. It automatically adjusts to maintain a comfortable environment, eliminating the need for manual adjustments and ensuring energy efficiency.

Safety is a top priority with the inclusion of a high-temperature safety shut-off. This feature prevents overheating and ensures safe, worry-free operation. The heater is also built to last, featuring durable US-made construction and a 2-year warranty for long-term reliability.

Ideal for rooms up to 500 square feet, the Cadet Com-Pak Twin Wall Heater is easy to install with a simple horizontal mounting setup that requires only two screws. Its clean white finish blends seamlessly into your décor, adding a modern touch to your space.

Whether you need a high-performance heating unit for a spacious area or a reliable and safe option for daily use, this Cadet heater delivers value through its efficiency, flexibility, and long-lasting quality.
