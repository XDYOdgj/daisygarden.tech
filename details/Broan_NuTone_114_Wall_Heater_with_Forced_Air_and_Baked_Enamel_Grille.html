Broan-NuTone 114 Wall Heater

The Broan-NuTone 114 Wall Heater is designed to provide efficient and reliable heating in any room, especially in spaces where space is limited. With its sleek white baked enamel grille and one-piece construction, it combines aesthetic appeal with long-lasting durability. This unit is perfect for small areas like hallways, bathrooms, or even cozy bedrooms.

The vertical louvers of the heater ensure that warm air is distributed evenly across the entire width of the unit, offering uniform comfort throughout the room. Its efficient steel sheathed heating element delivers quick heat output without long warm-up times, making it ideal for on-demand heating solutions. Pairing it with optional thermostat or timer models allows for a more personalized and energy-efficient experience.

Engineered with a quiet, thermally protected motor, this heater ensures safe and noise-free operation. The built-in transverse blower enhances air circulation, allowing heat to reach every corner of the room effectively. A great choice for anyone looking for a reliable, compact, and energy-conscious heating option.

Heater Features
  • White baked enamel grille with one-piece design for long-lasting durability
  • Vertical louvers ensure even heat distribution across the entire unit
  • Optional thermostat or timer for precise temperature control
  • Efficient steel sheathed heating element for quick and comfortable heating
  • Quiet motor with thermal protection and transverse blower for optimal air circulation
