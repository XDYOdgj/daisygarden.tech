ESTEAM Fibre Rinse

A premium carpet rinse agent designed to ensure deep cleaning without damaging your floors.

What Makes ESTEAM Fibre Rinse Special?

ESTEAM Fibre Rinse is an advanced acidic rinse solution that removes the remaining alkalinity after carpet cleaning. This helps to preserve the integrity of fibers and dyes, ensuring your carpets maintain a fresh, vibrant look for longer. It is formulated with a safe diluted pH of 2.4, making it gentle enough for use on both carpets and upholstery that can be cleaned with water.

Why Choose ESTEAM Fibre Rinse?

Whether you're working in a commercial setting or a home, this powerful product is essential for achieving optimal cleaning results. Its acidic properties help to break down tough stains like urine and tannin, while its dilution ratio of 1:128 ensures you can use it efficiently without over-saturation. Stain-resistant carpets benefit most from its formulation, which helps to stabilize colors and prevent browning.
